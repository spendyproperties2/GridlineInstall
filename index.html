Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

Are you licensed and insured?
Yes, we are fully insured for your protection. Our Georgia business license number is #123456789. We carry comprehensive liability insurance to protect your property during our services.
What forms of payment do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital payments (Zelle/Venmo) for your convenience. Payment is due upon completion of work unless otherwise arranged for commercial clients.
What is your service area?
We proudly serve Northeast Metro Atlanta: Johns Creek, Alpharetta, Suwanee, Duluth, Cumming, Lawrenceville, and surrounding communities. Contact us if you're outside these areas - we may still be able to help!
Do I need to provide any equipment?
We provide all necessary tools and basic mounting hardware. For TV mounts, you can provide your own or we can source a high-quality one for you at an additional cost. For other services, we'll discuss any needed materials during the booking process.
How long does a typical installation take?
Most TV mounting jobs take 1-2 hours. Smart home device installations typically take 1-2 hours per device. Furniture assembly times vary greatly depending on the item - a simple bookcase might take 30 minutes while a complex bed frame could take 2-3 hours. We'll provide a time estimate when you book.
Do you offer warranties on your work?
Yes, we offer a 90-day warranty on all our workmanship. If anything we installed becomes loose or has issues due to our installation, we'll return and fix it at no additional cost. This doesn't cover issues with the equipment itself, which would be covered by the manufacturer's warranty.
